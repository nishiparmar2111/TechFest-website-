About TechFest

TechFest 2026 is a cyber-themed technical festival organized for students to explore the world of technology, coding, and innovation.

This event provides a platform for participants to showcase their skills, learn new technologies, and collaborate with other developers.

The fest includes multiple activities like coding competitions, hackathons, technical quizzes, and workshops conducted by experts.

Our main goal is to encourage creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork among students.
